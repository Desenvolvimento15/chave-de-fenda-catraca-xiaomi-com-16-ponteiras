High torque ratchet with 3 settings Improved work efficiency The ratchet lets you rotate continuously in one direction without having to frequently change your grip. Capable of delivering torque up to 20 N⋅m*, the ratchet is robust, durable, labor-saving and efficient.
Turn it to the R position to turn screws to the right to tighten them*. Turn it to the middle locked position to tighten or loosen screws freely*. Turn it to the L position to turn screws to the left to loosen them*.
The figures above and the instructions for the R and L positions apply only to commonly used right-handed screws.
45-tooth high precision ratchet Smooth operation Featuring an all aluminium shell and 45-tooth precision gear, the ratchet rotates more smoothly for greater ease of operation and a better overall experience.
